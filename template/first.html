This survey is being undertaken towards Roman Klapaukh's PhD in the Software Engineering group at Victoria University of Wellington. The survey looks at how people perceive the symmetry of network diagrams. The Victoria University Human Ethics Committee has granted ethics approval for this experiment.

Each question will ask you which of two images you consider to be more symmetrical. No information about participants will be recorded apart from the answers. Thank you for your participation in the study.
